Heart Stroke Attack

A stroke occurs when the blood supply to part of your brain is interrupted or reduced, preventing brain tissue from getting oxygen and nutrients. Brain cells begin to die within minutes. Early detection and prevention are crucial for better outcomes.

Mediease - Heart-stroke predicting Machine learning model

Mediease is a web application dedicated to predicting the likelihood of a heart stroke. Our goal is to provide users with valuable insights into their health and enable early intervention when necessary. The prediction is based on a model trained with relevant health data, contributing to a proactive approach to healthcare.
